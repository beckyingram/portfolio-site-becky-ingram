About Me

Hi there, I'm Becky.

I'm a UX designer and researcher. My interest in UX was sparked by my previous experience conducting scientific research on relationships people have with nature. My background gave me a strong foundation in research and communication skills, fine-tuned my ability to create and follow procedures, and stay organized amidst chaotic atmospheres. I regularly worked with many stakeholders in diverse settings, balancing competing goals along the way. I shifted into UX because it offers more of the creative and technical challenges that I really enjoy. UX allows me to do two of my favorite things: ask questions and remain curious.
